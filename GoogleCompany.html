About Android XR

Android XR is Google's strategic initiative to build a comprehensive platform for extended reality (XR) experiences. It aims to leverage the openness, scale, and familiarity of the Android operating system to foster a vibrant ecosystem of immersive applications and hardware. By collaborating with key industry partners like Samsung and Qualcomm, Google intends to enable a new generation of devices that seamlessly blend digital content with the physical world, opening up new possibilities for communication, productivity, entertainment, and learning.
