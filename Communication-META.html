What is Meta AI?

Meta AI is a personal artificial intelligence assistant developed by Meta, built with their advanced Llama 4 model. It's designed to be conversational, helpful, and integrated across Meta's family of apps and devices. People can interact with Meta AI through voice or text for a variety of tasks.

Key features include:

  • Conversational Interaction: Designed for natural, seamless voice and text conversations.
  • Personalization: Learns user preferences and interests to provide more relevant and helpful answers and recommendations.
  • Problem Solving & Information: Can help solve problems, answer daily questions, and provide information by searching the web.
  • Image Generation & Editing: Integrated capabilities for creating and modifying images through conversation.
  • Discover Feed: A space to share and explore how others are using AI, see popular prompts, and remix them.

Meta AI Official Page

How Does It Work?

Seamless voice conversations with Meta AI.

Meta AI functions through a combination of advanced AI models, user data integration (with user control), and cross-platform accessibility:

  1. Llama 4 Model: The underlying AI model, Llama 4, powers Meta AI, enabling more personal, relevant, and conversational responses.
  2. Voice Technology: Features improved voice interaction, including a demo of full-duplex speech technology for a more natural voice experience where the AI generates voice directly.
  3. Personalization Engine: Meta AI can remember user-specified information (e.g., hobbies, interests) and pick up details from context. It can also draw on information shared on Meta products (like profile, liked content) to tailor responses, with this feature available in the US and Canada.
  4. Cross-Platform Integration: Accessible across Meta's ecosystem including WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, Messenger, a standalone Meta AI app, Ray-Ban Meta glasses, and the web. Users can sometimes switch between devices, like starting a conversation on glasses and continuing in the app.
  5. Web Search Integration: Can search the web to provide recommendations, deep dive on topics, and stay connected.

The standalone Meta AI app also merges with the Meta View companion app for Ray-Ban Meta glasses, centralizing AI experiences.

When Was It Introduced?

Meta has been integrating AI into its products for years. The latest significant update is the launch of a new standalone Meta AI app built with Llama 4, announced as a first step towards a more personal AI. The provided article indicates this launch around April 2025.

Key milestones include:

  • Ongoing AI Integration: Meta AI has been available across WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Messenger.
  • Standalone App Launch (Llama 4): Introduction of the new Meta AI app, marking an evolution with Llama 4 for more personal and conversational experiences.
  • Ray-Ban Meta Glasses Integration: Merging the Meta AI app with the Meta View companion app for Ray-Ban Meta glasses.
  • Web Interface Upgrade: Enhancements to Meta AI on the web, including voice interactions and the Discover feed.

The rollout of specific features like full-duplex voice demo and personalized responses started in select regions.

Meta AI app launch announcement.

Where Is It Implemented?

Meta AI accessible across multiple platforms.

Meta AI is being integrated across Meta's ecosystem and is available in various forms and regions:

  • Platforms:
    • Messaging Apps: WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook, and Messenger.
    • Standalone App: The new Meta AI app.
    • Wearables: Ray-Ban Meta glasses (with the Meta AI app serving as the companion app).
    • Web: Meta AI on the web (desktop).
  • Regional Availability (for specific features):
    • Voice Conversations (including full-duplex demo): Initially available in the US, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand.
    • Personalized Responses: Available in the US and Canada.

The goal is to make Meta AI easily accessible wherever users interact with Meta products, providing a seamless assistant experience across devices.
