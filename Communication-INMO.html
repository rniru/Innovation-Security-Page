What are INMO AR Glasses?

INMO AR Glasses are designed to be lightweight, stylish, and wearable augmented reality devices for everyday use. They aim to seamlessly integrate digital information and smart functionalities into the user's field of view, offering a hands-free experience. Key products like the INMO Air 2 and INMO Go showcase this vision by providing standalone AR capabilities without needing to be tethered to a smartphone.

Core goals for INMO AR glasses include:

  • Everyday Wearability: Focusing on comfort and style, making them suitable for extended use.
  • Standalone Operation: Running on an Android-based OS (INMO OS), allowing them to function independently.
  • Smart Assistance: Providing features like navigation, translation, notifications, and AI-powered interactions (e.g., INMO GPT).
  • Intuitive Interaction: Offering controls through touch gestures, voice commands, and potentially head movements.

INMO Air 2 offers a stylish and functional AR experience.

How Do They Work?

INMO Go is designed to be a featherlight smart assistant.

INMO AR Glasses operate as standalone devices, typically running a customized Android-based operating system (like INMO OS 2.0 on the Air 2). They project information onto dual full-color Micro-OLED displays, creating an augmented view for the user.

Key technological features include:

  1. Processing Power: Equipped with mobile processors like Qualcomm Snapdragon series (e.g., W5+ Gen 1 in INMO Go, a 4-core CPU in Air 2).
  2. Connectivity: Built-in Wi-Fi and Bluetooth for internet access and pairing with other devices.
  3. Sensors & Camera: Include accelerometers, gyroscopes, and cameras (e.g., 8MP RGB camera on Air 2) for environmental awareness, interaction, and media capture.
  4. Interaction Methods: Users interact via a touchpad on the temple, voice commands, and in some cases, limited hand gestures.
  5. AI Integration: Models like INMO Go feature INMO GPT, an AI assistant for enhanced contextual understanding and task execution.
  6. Audio: Open ear audio systems or dual stereo speakers deliver sound without blocking external hearing.
  7. App Ecosystem: An app store (like INMOVERSE) provides access to various applications for navigation, translation, messaging, social media, and entertainment.

They are also designed to be compatible with prescription lenses, enhancing accessibility.

Where Will They Be Implemented & Used?

INMO AR glasses are designed for various daily activities. (Image is illustrative)

INMO AR Glasses are intended for a wide range of everyday applications, enhancing how users interact with information and their surroundings:

  • Smart Notifications & Communication: View messages, take calls, and stay updated without reaching for a phone.
  • Navigation: Receive turn-by-turn directions directly in the field of view.
  • Real-time Translation: Translate spoken or written language on the go.
  • Teleprompter: Assist with speeches or presentations by displaying notes discreetly.
  • Media Consumption: Watch videos or listen to music.
  • Photography & Videography: Capture moments hands-free using the built-in camera.
  • Light Productivity: Accessing information, notes, or using specific AR applications for tasks.

The goal is to make these glasses a natural extension of a user's digital life, providing convenient, contextual information and tools in a wearable form factor.
