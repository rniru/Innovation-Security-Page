About INMO

INMO is dedicated to making augmented reality accessible and practical for everyday users. They focus on creating AR glasses that are not only technologically advanced but also lightweight, stylish, and comfortable for daily wear. With a world-class team, INMO develops its own hardware, software (INMO OS), and an ecosystem of applications (INMOVERSE) to deliver a seamless and intuitive AR experience. Their goal is to integrate digital information and smart functionalities naturally into the user's view.

Insights from INMO's CEO

Mr. Yang Longsheng, CEO of INMO

"Our goal and vision all along have been to make AR glasses lightweight and everyday and wireless... we believe that what consumers really want or expect is glasses that are like earphones or watches, which can be worn at any time, are very light, and are similar to our daily glasses.”

Read Full Interview

Mr. Yang Longsheng emphasizes that INMO's core philosophy is to create AR glasses that seamlessly integrate into daily life, much like smartphones or smartwatches, but with the added benefit of keeping users present in their surroundings. The INMO Air2, for example, is designed as a "lightweight, wireless, and everyday wearable device."

He envisions these glasses as intelligent assistants, akin to "Jarvis" from Iron Man, capable of performing tasks like real-time translation for smoother communication during travel or video conferences, and acting as a teleprompter for presentations. Beyond work, INMO aims for their AR glasses to be life companions, assisting with everyday tasks like cooking by displaying recipes directly in the user's view, or guiding home repairs. Entertainment, such as watching YouTube on the go, is also a key aspect, offering a hands-free experience.

"We are the first company in the industry to use a watch chip to make [AR glasses]," Mr. Yang stated, highlighting INMO's commitment to convenience and an unobtrusive product.
