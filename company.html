About Huawei

Huawei is a Chinese multinational technology corporation headquartered in Shenzhen, Guangdong. It is renowned for its contributions to the fields of telecommunications, consumer electronics, and digital technology solutions. The company initially gained recognition as a provider of networking and telecommunications equipment but has since diversified into producing smartphones, tablets, laptops, and other consumer tech. Operating in over 170 countries, Huawei has become a global giant in the tech industry, despite facing challenges such as trade restrictions in certain markets.

Ren Zhengfei

Founder & CEO

"We will embrace the future by building an intelligent world where all things are connected."

Learn More

The founder of Huawei is Ren Zhengfei, a former engineer in the People's Liberation Army of China. He established the company in 1987 with a vision to develop a Chinese alternative to foreign telecommunications infrastructure. Through his leadership, Huawei evolved from a small reseller of telephone exchange equipment into one of the world's most influential tech companies. Ren's strategic focus on research and development helped Huawei become a leader in 5G technology and mobile innovation.

Driving the Future

As of 2025, Huawei continues to push boundaries with advanced devices. Notably, the Huawei Mate XT Ultimate Design stands as the world's first commercial trifold smartphone, offering a massive 10.2-inch OLED display and powered by the Kirin 9010 chipset. Other recent releases include the Huawei Mate X6, a high-end foldable phone with a powerful 50MP camera system, and the Huawei Pura70 series, which features improved camera technology and modern aesthetics. Huawei has also introduced the Mate 70 series, significant for using HarmonyOS Next—an operating system that replaces Android on its devices in China. In addition, the Huawei Watch 5 was launched with enhanced health monitoring features, and the MatePad Pro 13.2-inch tablet was released with a PaperMatte screen and creative tools for professionals.
