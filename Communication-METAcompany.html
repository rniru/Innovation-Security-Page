The Meta Story

2004

Facebook Founded

Mark Zuckerberg launched Facebook from his Harvard dorm room, beginning the journey of what would eventually become Meta.

2021

Rebranding to Meta

Facebook Inc. rebranded as Meta Platforms, Inc., signaling a strategic shift toward building the metaverse and expanding beyond social media.

2023

Llama AI Models

Meta released its Llama large language models to researchers, advancing open-source AI development and laying groundwork for future AI assistants.

2025

Meta AI App Launch

Introduction of the standalone Meta AI app built with Llama 4, marking a significant step toward more personal AI assistants integrated across platforms.

About Meta

Meta Platforms, Inc. (formerly Facebook, Inc.) is a global technology company focused on building technologies that help people connect, find communities, and grow businesses. The company has evolved from its social media roots to become a leader in AI, virtual reality, and augmented reality technologies. Meta's mission extends beyond social networking to creating immersive experiences and developing AI assistants that enhance how people interact with technology and each other.

Meta's AI Vision

Meta is leveraging its decades of experience in personalizing user experiences to create more intelligent and helpful AI assistants. The company's approach to AI focuses on making technology more conversational, personal, and seamlessly integrated into daily life. With innovations like full-duplex speech technology for more natural voice interactions and cross-platform integration, Meta is working to ensure AI assistants can understand context and provide relevant assistance across devices and applications.
